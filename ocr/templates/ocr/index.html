Quick rhythm

Choose a mode, add an image, extract the text, then listen or copy it. Everything on this page is built around that one flow.

Hands-free operation

Use voice commands or keyboard shortcuts for complete accessibility:

  • Say "scan" to open camera and capture
  • Say "read latest" to hear extracted text
  • Say "history" to access previous scans
  • Say "activate voice" to turn on voice commands
  • Press Alt+V to activate voice commands manually if needed
  • Press Alt+R to read aloud
